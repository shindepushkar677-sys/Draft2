I believe art shouldn't feel intimidating or "too perfect." It should feel expressive, relatable, and something people instantly connect with. Most days, you'll find me covered in paint, obsessing over textures, and convincing myself that one more brushstroke will fix everything. Usually, it does.

Hi, I'm Rachana Shirkande, an artist who fell deeply in love with paint, textures, and giant blank walls. What started as a creative escape slowly turned into a full-time passion.

From large-scale wall murals and textured finishes to canvas paintings and experimental art pieces, I love creating anything that brings ideas to life.

The artist in me loves experimenting, getting messy, and making things feel alive and believe everyone has a fond for art. Hoping to see you on the other side!!
