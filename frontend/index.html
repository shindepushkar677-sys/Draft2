THE ARTIST

Art, to me, is what makes a space feel remembered.

Hi, I’m Rachana Shirkande — an artist passionate about transforming blank surfaces into immersive, expressive spaces through murals, textures, and handcrafted artworks. With a background in architecture and over five years of creative experience, my work blends spatial storytelling, material exploration, and artistic emotion to create pieces that feel thoughtful, tactile, and deeply connected to their environment. From large-scale wall murals and canvas paintings to experimental texture art and custom wardrobe finishes, every project is designed to bring individuality, warmth, and character into a space.
